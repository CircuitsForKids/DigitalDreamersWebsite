The goal of this game is to introduce the topics that will be learned in the rest of the module one games.
To navigate the game, click play game from the home screen and then answer the questions that you think are correct.
In order to move on to the next lesson and unlock more customizable features for your lightbulb character,
you will need to answer a minimum of 5 questions correctly. Good luck and have fun!
