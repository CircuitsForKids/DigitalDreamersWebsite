Here is our first game, Flappy Battery

We hope you are as excited as we are about learning digital circuits!

Flappy Battery is a game similar to the popular mobile game Flappy Bird.
In order to play, press the space bar button on your keyboard to make the lightbulb jump up.
One press is enough to make the lightbulb jump pretty high so try to time it right.
The game will keep track of your highest score, good luck and have fun!
